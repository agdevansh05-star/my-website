Our Story, Our Pride

Two and a half decades of nourishing families with purity, passion, and an unwavering commitment to quality.

Our Foundation

From a Small Kitchen to a Trusted National Brand

MOM'S PRIDE was born in 2000 in the foothills of the Himalayas, inspired by a mother's simple principle: never compromise on what you feed your family. What started as a small atta-milling unit has grown into one of India's most trusted food manufacturing companies.

Today, our state-of-the-art facility in Siliguri spans over 50,000 sq. ft., employing 400+ skilled workers, and serves families across 18 states. Yet our core philosophy remains unchanged — every product must meet the standards a loving mother would set.

We source directly from farmers, process with zero harmful additives, and test every batch in our in-house laboratories before it reaches your kitchen. That is the MOM'S PRIDE promise.
